# BL/GL Novel Structure Template

## Genre Characteristics
- Same-sex romantic relationship at center
- Emotional depth สำคัญมาก
- Chemistry และ tension
- Character development
- Social/personal obstacles
- Can range from sweet to explicit

## Recommended Length
- **Chapters:** 20-30 บท
- **Words/Chapter:** 3,500-5,500 คำ
- **Total:** 70,000-165,000 คำ

---

## ACT 1: THE MEETING (บทที่ 1-6)

### Chapter 1-2: Establishing Lives
**Goals:**
- แนะนำ protagonist และชีวิตของเขา/เธอ
- Establish personality, fears, desires
- Show world and relationships
- Hint at what's missing

**Character Notes:**
- ไม่จำเป็นต้อง "out" ตั้งแต่แรก
- สามารถ explore identity ได้
- หรือ established ตั้งแต่ต้น

### Chapter 3-4: The Meeting
**Goals:**
- First encounter ระหว่าง leads
- สร้าง impression (บวก/ลบ/ซับซ้อน)
- Chemistry must spark
- Establish dynamic

**Meeting Types:**
- Strangers to lovers
- Friends to lovers
- Rivals to lovers
- Boss/employee
- Senpai/Kouhai

### Chapter 5-6: The Situation
**Goals:**
- Establish why they'll keep meeting
- First hints of attraction (maybe denied)
- Introduce obstacles
- Set up conflict

---

## ACT 2A: GROWING CLOSER (บทที่ 7-14)

### Chapter 7-9: Forced Proximity
**Goals:**
- More time together
- Discover layers of each other
- Tension builds
- Attraction harder to ignore

**Romantic Progression:**
- Awareness of the other
- Thinking about them
- Finding excuses to be near
- Jealousy (subtle)

### Chapter 10-12: Emotional Intimacy
**Goals:**
- Vulnerable moments
- Share backstories
- Understand each other
- First intimate moment (can be subtle)

### Chapter 13-14: Midpoint
**Goals:**
- Confession หรือ first kiss
- Or major step forward
- Stakes change
- Relationship acknowledged (even if just between them)

---

## ACT 2B: CHALLENGES (บทที่ 15-21)

### Chapter 15-17: Navigating Together
**Goals:**
- Figure out relationship
- Face external pressures
- Internal doubts
- Deepen connection

**Potential Obstacles:**
- Family expectations
- Social pressure
- Coming out issues
- Career conflicts
- Past relationships
- Fear of commitment
- Misunderstanding

### Chapter 18-19: The Crisis
**Goals:**
- Major obstacle emerges
- Secret revealed
- Misunderstanding
- External force separates them

### Chapter 20-21: The Separation
**Goals:**
- They're apart
- Both suffer
- Realize depth of feelings
- Must decide what to fight for

---

## ACT 3: THE RESOLUTION (บทที่ 22-28)

### Chapter 22-23: Fighting for Love
**Goals:**
- Decision to fight for relationship
- Overcome internal obstacles
- Prepare for external battles

### Chapter 24-26: Confronting Obstacles
**Goals:**
- Face external pressures
- Stand up for relationship
- Support from allies
- Grand gesture (if appropriate)

### Chapter 27-28: Happy Ending
**Goals:**
- Together
- Obstacles resolved
- Future established
- Satisfying conclusion

## CONTENT RATINGS

### Sweet/Fluffy
- Focus on emotions
- Fade to black intimate scenes
- Light conflicts
- Comfort read

### Mature
- Emotional depth
- Some physical intimacy described
- Heavier themes possible
- Complex relationships

### Explicit
- Detailed intimate scenes
- Still need emotional connection
- Consent always clear
- Romance not just physical

---

## RELATIONSHIP DYNAMICS

### Classic BL Dynamics
- Seme/Uke (แต่ไม่จำเป็นต้อง rigid)
- Cold x Warm
- Tsundere x Patient
- Rivals to Lovers
- Childhood friends

### Classic GL Dynamics
- Femme x Femme
- Butch x Femme
- Cool x Cute
- Senpai x Kouhai
- Best friends to lovers

### Modern Approach
- Flexible dynamics
- Both characters have agency
- Power balance
- Mutual respect

---

## EMOTIONAL BEATS

### Key Romantic Moments
1. First awareness of attraction
2. First meaningful conversation
3. First vulnerable moment
4. First touch (intentional)
5. First almost-moment
6. First kiss
7. First "I love you"
8. First obstacle overcome together
9. Commitment moment

### Tension Building
```
Awareness → Denial → Acceptance → Acting on it

ตัวอย่าง:
บทที่ 5: สังเกตว่าเขาน่าสนใจ
บทที่ 8: ปฏิเสธว่าไม่ได้ชอบ
บทที่ 12: ยอมรับกับตัวเองว่าชอบ
บทที่ 15: แสดงออก
```

## HANDLING SENSITIVE TOPICS

### Coming Out
- ทุกคนมี journey ต่างกัน
- ไม่ต้อง tragic
- Support system สำคัญ
- Can be positive experience

### Family Acceptance
- Range of reactions realistic
- Not all families reject
- Growth possible
- Found family valid

### Social Issues
- Can address discrimination
- Balance with hope
- Not define entire story
- Handle with care

---

## TIPS FOR BL/GL

### Do's
- ✅ Give both leads agency
- ✅ Make attraction mutual
- ✅ Show chemistry in small moments
- ✅ Develop both characters equally
- ✅ Create unique relationship dynamic

### Don'ts
- ❌ Fetishize the relationship
- ❌ Make one character passive object
- ❌ Use harmful stereotypes
- ❌ Rely only on physical attraction
- ❌ Ignore consent

## DIALOGUE EXAMPLES

### Building Tension
```
"ทำไมคุณมองฉันแบบนั้น?"
"แบบไหน?"
"แบบ... ไม่รู้สิ ฉันอธิบายไม่ถูก"
"บางทีคุณก็รู้ แค่ไม่กล้าพูด"
```

### Vulnerable Moment
```
"ฉันไม่เคยรู้สึกแบบนี้กับใคร"
"แบบไหน?"
"แบบที่... อยากให้เวลาหยุด ตอนอยู่กับคุณ"
```

### Confession
```
"ฉันว่าเราควรหยุดแกล้งทำเป็นว่าไม่มีอะไร"
"หมายความว่าอะไร?"
"หมายความว่า... ฉันชอบคุณ มากกว่าเพื่อน มากกว่าที่ควรจะเป็น"
```
